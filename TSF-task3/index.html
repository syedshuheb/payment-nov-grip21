What is the Helping Hands Club?

As the COVID-19 crisis continues to loom over us, The Government of India has taken a strict combat measure by directing a lockdown of the entire nation. At this great hour of need, We have been supporting our NGO partners providing Covid-relief and trying to ease the suffering of families caught in the eye of the pandemic storm - with donations. Our NGOs in close coordination with State Governments & District Administration, have stepped in to provide relief by providing food, unused meds and other necessities to thousands of people across the country who can't afford it.

Our website will undertake the operations with an unwavering dedication, collects donations from people and provide timely, healthy meals with a focus on combining nutrition with hygiene to the ones in need.
